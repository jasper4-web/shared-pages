Can I upgrade or downgrade later?
Anytime. Most owners start with Starter or Growth and move up as the AI proves itself. Upgrades are usually live within days — no new setup fee to move up a tier.
Is there a contract? Can I cancel?
There's an initial 3-month commitment so the system has time to work. After that, cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No long-term contracts.
Who owns my data?
You do — always. Your leads, contacts, call history, and reviews are yours. If you ever leave, you keep your data.
