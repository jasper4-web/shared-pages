Pricing questions

The details, up front

Why is there a setup fee?
The setup fee stands your department up — we learn your business, shape your industry blueprint to it, write and tune how it talks, connect it to how you already work, and test it with you. After that, the monthly keeps it running and current. Setup fees are not refundable once the work starts.
Can I get my money back if it isn't working?
There's a 30-day money-back guarantee on the monthly fee. After the minimum term you can cancel with 30 days notice.
Can I move up or down a level?
Any time. Most owners start at Starter or Growth and move up once they see the calendar filling. There's no new setup fee to move up.
Is there a discount for paying yearly?
Yes — pay for twelve months up front and one month is free.
Who owns my customer information?
You do — always. Your customers, call history, messages and reviews are yours. If you ever leave, we hand it all over.
Do I have to learn anything?
No. That's the entire point of paying us instead of buying a tool. If you can answer a phone, you're covered.
