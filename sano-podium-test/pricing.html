Pricing questions

The details, up front

Why is there a setup fee?
The setup fee stands your department up — we learn your business, shape your industry blueprint to it, write and tune how it talks, connect it to how you already work, and test it with you. After that, the monthly keeps it running and current. Setup fees are not refundable once the work starts.
Can I get my money back if it isn't working?
Within the first 30 days you can walk away: we refund that month's fee and release you from the minimum term. The one-time setup fee is not refundable once we've started building, so on Growth that means you'd get the $999 back and the $2,497 setup stays spent. After 30 days the minimum term applies (three months on most plans, six on Total Transformation), and after that you can cancel any time with 30 days notice.
Can I move up or down a level?
Any time. Most owners start at Starter or Growth and move up once they see the calendar filling. There's no new setup fee to move up.
Is there a discount for paying yearly?
Yes — you pay for eleven months up front and the twelfth is free. If you cancel mid-year we refund the unused months beyond your minimum term.
Who owns my customer information?
You do — always. Your customers, call history, messages and reviews are yours. If you ever leave, we hand it all over.
What happens if I go over my included minutes?
Calls don't stop. If you hit the cap mid-month it falls back to text-back and your own line until you approve more minutes, and we tell you before it becomes a bill. If you're consistently running over, we'll either move you up a level or add a clearly-priced block of minutes — your choice, agreed in advance. We won't bill you for overage you haven't agreed to in advance.
Do I keep my own phone number?
Yes. Your number stays yours. We can either forward it to the system or set up a new line alongside it — whichever you prefer. If you ever leave, the number goes with you.
How much of my time does setup actually take?
About two hours of yours, total — spread across a kickoff conversation, a look at what we've written for you, and a final sign-off. The 14 to 75 days is our build time, not your workload.
Is ad spend included in the price?
No, and we'd rather be blunt about it. On Scale and above we plan and manage your ads, but the media budget is paid to Google or Meta directly and is separate from what you pay us. We'll help you set a sensible number and you keep control of it.
Do I have to learn anything?
No. That's the entire point of paying us instead of buying a tool. If you can answer a phone, you're covered.
