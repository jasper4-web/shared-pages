Questions

Dental & Health, answered

Does it replace our front desk?
No. It covers what they cannot get to — calls while they are busy, after-hours inquiries, and recall outreach.
Can it handle recall?
Yes. Recall and overdue-visit reminders run on a schedule you approve.
How do you handle patient privacy and HIPAA?
Straight answer: the assistant collects the name, contact details and the reason for the call. For a practice that reason counts as protected health information, so it is handled that way — it is not used for anything else and it is not sent anywhere it should not go. The assistant does not ask for clinical history, and anything sensitive is routed to your team rather than handled automatically. If you are a covered practice, we sign a business associate agreement before anything touches patient information — no agreement, no launch.
Is the texting compliant? I don't want trouble.
It matters most when you are running recall. Here is how we handle it, rather than a blanket yes. Marketing texts only go to people who have given written permission. Every one has a clear way to stop, and we honor it immediately. We register your number for business texting properly instead of blasting from an unregistered line. We are not your lawyers, and we will decline a campaign we think puts you at risk.
You haven't built a Dental & Health one yet — am I the experiment?
Straight answer: you'd be among the first in this trade, and we're not going to pretend otherwise. Here's what protects you. You approve a written blueprint before we build anything — that document is the spec we're held to. The setup fee is split half at kickoff and half at go-live, and you don't owe the balance if we miss the spec. The money-back guarantee runs 30 days from go-live, not from signing, so a longer build never eats it. And the trades we have built (HVAC, plumbing, roofing) share most of the machinery — what changes is the language and the timing, not the engine.
You’re new — why should I trust you with my phone?
Fair question, and we’d rather say it plainly: we’re early, so we don’t have a wall of customer logos to show you. What we do have is a clear process, nothing going live until you approve it, a money-back guarantee that runs 30 days from go-live (not from signing), a setup fee split half at kickoff and half at go-live that you don't owe the balance of if we miss your approved blueprint, and no long lock-in. Judge us on the demo.
What if SANO doesn’t make it?
Fair thing to ask a new company. Your phone number is forwarded, never ported away from you — you keep ownership of it the whole time, and switching it back is a phone call to your carrier. Your customer list, call history and messages are exported to you on request at any time, not just if you cancel. Nothing we run creates a dependency you can’t unwind in a day.
What happens if it goes down while I’m on a job?
Calls fall back to ringing your normal line, so the worst case is the situation you’re in today — not a dead number. We monitor it and you get told if something breaks, rather than finding out from a customer.
