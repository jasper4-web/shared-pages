Questions

How this actually works

Will it sound like a real person?
It sounds like a friendly, professional person on your team, in English or Spanish. It follows what you've approved and never makes things up about your business. It also says it's an assistant at the start of every conversation — not only when someone asks.
What if it doesn't know an answer?
It takes the details and passes them straight to you instead of guessing. The customer still feels looked after, and you get what you need to call them back.
Do I have to use all of it?
No. Most owners start with the front desk and the follow-up, then add more once they see it working. You're picking how much of the department you want, not shopping a feature list.
What if it tells a customer something wrong?
It works from a script you approved and it refuses to quote prices or commit to anything outside it — anything else gets handed to you with the details. If it does get something wrong, every call is recorded and transcribed, so we can see exactly what happened. We correct it the same day, we call the customer back ourselves if you want us to, and if it booked a job it shouldn't have, we own that — you decide whether to honor it and we fix the script so it can't repeat.
What happens if it goes down while I'm on a job?
Calls fall back to ringing your normal line, so the worst case is the situation you're in today — not a dead number. We monitor it and you get told if something breaks, rather than finding out from a customer.
Does it work with what I already use?
Generally yes — your calendar, your listing, the way you already schedule. We fit around your process instead of making you change it.
