No. Start with what moves the needle most — usually the AI receptionist and missed-call text-back — and switch on the rest as you grow. It's one connected system, expanded at your pace.

Yes — Google Business, your calendar, common CRMs, payments, and more. For most owners we recommend our built-in platform because the AI is native to it.
