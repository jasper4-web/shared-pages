Questions

How this actually works

Is this software I have to set up myself?
No. We build it around your business, connect it to how you already work, test it with you, and then run it. You approve it once. If you can answer a phone, you're covered.
Will it sound like a real person?
It sounds like a friendly, professional person on your team, in English or Spanish. It follows what you've approved and never makes things up about your business.
What if it doesn't know an answer?
It takes the details and passes them straight to you instead of guessing. The customer still feels looked after, and you get what you need to call them back.
Do I have to use all of it?
No. Most owners start with the front desk and the follow-up, then add more once they see it working. You're picking how much of the department you want, not shopping a feature list.
Does it work with what I already use?
Generally yes — your calendar, your listing, the way you already schedule. We fit around your process instead of making you change it.
