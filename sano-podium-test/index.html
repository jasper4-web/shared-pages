Straight answers

What owners ask us first

Do I have to learn or manage anything?
No — that's the entire point of hiring us instead of buying a tool. We build it, run it, and keep it current. You approve how it works once, then go back to your business.
Will my customers know it's AI?
It sounds like a friendly, professional person on your team, in English or Spanish. It says it's an assistant at the start of every conversation — not only when asked. It works from what you've approved and hands off anything outside it, with the details, straight to you.
How is this different from the software I already pay for?
A lot of owners are paying for a tool they use a fraction of, because nobody has time to learn it. We're not selling you access — we're doing the work. If you already own something that works, we'll often run that instead of replacing it.
What does it cost, honestly?
It starts at $397/month plus a one-time fee to stand it up. That entry plan answers by text around the clock; live voice answering starts at Growth ($999/month). The right comparison isn't a low-cost monthly app you'd still have to run yourself — it's what a front-desk person, someone doing your marketing, and someone keeping the tech working would cost you. What if it tells a customer something wrong?
It works from a script you approved and refuses to quote prices or commit to anything outside it — everything else is handed to you with the details. Every call and message is recorded or logged, so we can see exactly what happened. We correct it the same day, call the customer back ourselves if you want, and if it booked a job it shouldn't have, we own that — you decide whether to honor it and we fix the script.
What happens if it goes down?
Calls fall back to ringing your normal line, so the worst case is the situation you're in today — not a dead number. We monitor it and tell you if something breaks, rather than you hearing it from a customer.
Am I locked into a contract?
Here's the honest arithmetic. The monthly fee carries a 30-day money-back guarantee. The one-time setup fee is not refundable once we start building, and plans run on a short minimum term (three months on most, six on the top tier) so the system has time to work. After that, cancel with 30 days notice. Your customer information is always yours to take with you.
What if SANO doesn't make it?
Fair thing to ask a new company. Your phone number is forwarded, never ported away from you — you keep ownership of it the whole time, and switching it back is a phone call to your carrier. Your customer list, call history and messages are exported to you on request at any time, not just if you cancel. Nothing we run creates a dependency you can't unwind in a day.
You're new — why should I trust you with my phone?
Fair question, and we'd rather say it plainly: we're early, so we don't have a wall of customer logos to show you. What we do have is a clear process, nothing going live until you approve it, a 30-day money-back guarantee on the monthly fee that also ends the minimum term, and no long lock-in.
