What owners ask us first

Do I have to learn or manage anything?
No — that's the entire point of hiring us instead of buying a tool. We build it, run it, and keep it current. You approve how it works once, then go back to your business.
Will my customers know it's AI?
It sounds like a friendly, professional person on your team, in English or Spanish. It identifies itself as an assistant when asked — and up front in states that require it. It only says what you've approved, and anything it can't answer comes straight to you with the details.
How is this different from the software I already pay for?
A lot of owners are paying for a tool they use a fraction of, because nobody has time to learn it. We're not selling you access — we're doing the work. If you already own something that works, we'll often run that instead of replacing it.
What does it cost, honestly?
It starts at $397/month plus a one-time fee to stand it up. That entry plan answers by text around the clock; live voice answering starts at Growth ($999/month). The right comparison isn't a low-cost monthly app you'd still have to run yourself — it's what a front-desk person, someone doing your marketing, and someone keeping the tech working would cost you. Am I locked into a contract?
Here's the honest arithmetic. The monthly fee carries a 30-day money-back guarantee. The one-time setup fee is not refundable once we start building, and plans run on a short minimum term (three months on most, six on the top tier) so the system has time to work. After that, cancel with 30 days notice. Your customer information is always yours to take with you.
You're new — why should I trust you with my phone?
Fair question, and we'd rather say it plainly: we're early, so we don't have a wall of customer logos to show you. What we do have is a clear process, nothing going live until you approve it, a money-back guarantee, and no long contract. Judge us on the demo.
