Terms of Service

These terms govern working with SANO Systems LLC. We've kept them short and readable on purpose.

Billing

Services are billed as a one-time setup fee to stand your department up — half at kickoff, half at go-live — plus a monthly subscription that begins at go-live. If we cannot deliver what your approved blueprint specifies, the balance is not payable. Setup fees are non-refundable once work begins. If you prepay, you pay for eleven months and the twelfth is free. Cancel mid-year and we refund the unused months beyond your minimum term.

Guarantee and cancellation

The 30 days run from go-live, not from signing, and billing starts on the same day. Cancelling within that window refunds that month's fee and ends the minimum term — you owe nothing further. After 30 days, plans carry an initial minimum term (three months on Starter, Growth and Scale, six on Total Transformation), after which you can cancel any time with 30 days notice. Your data is exported to you free of charge.
