Privacy Policy

SANO Systems LLC is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains, in plain English, what we collect and what we do with it.

What we collect

What you give us. Your name, business name, email, and phone number when you fill in a form, book a demo, or become a client.

What's collected automatically. Basic technical information such as your IP address, browser, device type, and which pages you visited, through standard analytics and cookies.

What comes from connected services. If you become a client, information from the services you ask us to connect on your behalf — for example your business listing or calendar.

How we use it

To provide and improve our services, to communicate with you about your demo or your account, to process payments, and to meet our legal obligations. We do not sell your information.

Your rights

You can ask us to show you the information we hold about you, correct it, or delete it. You can opt out of marketing messages at any time, ask for a portable export of your data, and withdraw consent. To make any of these requests, email us and we'll take care of it.

Calls, recordings and AI processing

Our service answers and handles phone calls and messages on behalf of our clients. That means:

  • Calls are recorded and transcribed so the conversation can be understood, actioned and reviewed for quality. Every call announces that it is recorded at the start — regardless of where the caller is. Consent rules vary by state, so we follow the strictest one that could apply — which is why we always announce, on every call.
  • The assistant says it is an assistant at the start of every conversation — not only when someone asks.
  • Conversation content is processed by third-party speech and language providers acting on our instructions. They are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.
  • Recordings and transcripts are retained only as long as needed to run the service and meet legal obligations, and are deleted on request where we are free to do so.

Text messaging

Texting is core to what we run, so we are specific about it:

  • Marketing texts go only to people who have given written permission. Service messages (appointment confirmations, reminders, replies to an inbound message) go to people who contacted the business.
  • Every marketing message includes a way to stop. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help. We honor opt-outs immediately.
  • Message and data rates may apply, and message frequency varies.
  • Mobile information is never shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Phone numbers collected for one business are never used for another.

Health-care clients

Where we work with a dental, chiropractic, veterinary, med-spa or other practice that is a covered entity, the call and message content we handle can include protected health information. In that case we sign a business associate agreement before anything touches patient information — no agreement, no launch. We collect only what is needed to book or route, we do not ask for clinical history, and anything sensitive is routed to the practice rather than handled automatically.

Client data ownership

If you're a client, your customers, conversations, call records and reviews belong to you — not to us. If you ever leave, we hand it all over in a usable format.
