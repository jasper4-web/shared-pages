Questions

Retail & Local Shops, answered

I do not take appointments. Is this still useful?
Yes. Answering questions instantly and staying in touch with past customers matters whether or not you book time slots.
Can it answer messages from social too?
Phone and text are what we run today, and they land in one place. Social messaging is not part of the service yet — we would rather say so than sell you something we have not built.
Do I have to run any of it?
No. We build it and run it. You approve how it sounds and then get on with your day.
You haven't built a Retail & Local Shops one yet — am I the experiment?
Straight answer: you'd be among the first in this trade, and we're not going to pretend otherwise. Here's what protects you. You approve a written blueprint before we build anything — that document is the spec we're held to. The setup fee is split half at kickoff and half at go-live, and you don't owe the balance if we miss the spec. The money-back guarantee runs 30 days from go-live, not from signing, so a longer build never eats it. And the trades we have built (HVAC, plumbing, roofing) share most of the machinery — what changes is the language and the timing, not the engine.
You’re new — why should I trust you with my phone?
Fair question, and we’d rather say it plainly: we’re early, so we don’t have a wall of customer logos to show you. What we do have is a clear process, nothing going live until you approve it, a money-back guarantee that runs 30 days from go-live (not from signing), a setup fee split half at kickoff and half at go-live that you don't owe the balance of if we miss your approved blueprint, and no long lock-in.
