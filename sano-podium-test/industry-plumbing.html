Questions

Plumbing, answered

Can it answer while I am on a job?
That is exactly when it works hardest. On Starter it texts back within seconds; from Growth up it picks up the phone live, whether you are free or elbow-deep under a sink.
Will customers know it is not me?
It sounds like a friendly, professional person on your team, and it speaks English and Spanish. Anything it cannot answer comes straight to you.
Does it help me get paid faster?
Yes — invoicing and pay-by-text come in at the Scale level. It sends the invoice and a secure pay-by-text link right after the job, instead of it waiting until you get home.
Is the texting compliant? I don't want trouble.
It matters most when you are texting after hours. Here is how we handle it, rather than a blanket yes. Marketing texts only go to people who have given written permission. Every one has a clear way to stop, and we honor it immediately. We register your number for business texting properly instead of blasting from an unregistered line. We are not your lawyers, and we will decline a campaign we think puts you at risk.
You’re new — why should I trust you with my phone?
Fair question, and we’d rather say it plainly: we’re early, so we don’t have a wall of customer logos to show you. What we do have is a clear process, nothing going live until you approve it, a 30-day money-back guarantee on the monthly fee that also ends the minimum term (setup is non-refundable once we start), and no long lock-in. Judge us on the demo.
What if SANO doesn’t make it?
Fair thing to ask a new company. Your phone number is forwarded, never ported away from you — you keep ownership of it the whole time, and switching it back is a phone call to your carrier. Your customer list, call history and messages are exported to you on request at any time, not just if you cancel. Nothing we run creates a dependency you can’t unwind in a day.
What happens if it goes down while I’m on a job?
Calls fall back to ringing your normal line, so the worst case is the situation you’re in today — not a dead number. We monitor it and you get told if something breaks, rather than finding out from a customer.
