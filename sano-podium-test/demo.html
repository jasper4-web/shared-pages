Before you ask

What to expect

Is this a sales pitch?
It's a working demo. We show you the department we'd run for your business and what it would cost. If it isn't a fit, we'll say so — we'd rather not take on work we can't do well.
How long does it take?
About 15 minutes. If you want to go deeper on your industry's blueprint we'll happily use more, but 15 is enough to know whether it's worth continuing.
Do I need to prepare anything?
No. It helps if you know roughly how many calls you miss and when your busiest hours are, but we can work that out together.
What if I already pay for something similar?
Tell us what you have. If it works and you like it, we'll often run that for you instead of replacing it. The value we add is doing the work, not selling you another subscription.
Will you spam me afterwards?
No. We'll reach out to set a time, and follow up once if we don't hear back. Tell us to stop and we stop.
