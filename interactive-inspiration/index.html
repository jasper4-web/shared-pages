02

Make It Yours — The Buildable Toolkit

the interactive moves we CAN ship on a client site

Every showpiece above is really just a few of these techniques stacked up. Used one or two at a time, with restraint, these are exactly what take a client demo from nice to "whoa." Each links to the tool. Difficulty is honest.

Buttery smooth scrollEasy
Instantly makes a site feel high-end — momentum scrolling instead of choppy jumps. The fastest "premium" upgrade there is.
with Lenis ↗ · one script tag
Scroll reveals · pin · scrubMedium
Elements fade/slide in, sections pin while content animates, images scrub frame-by-frame to scroll. The backbone of scroll-storytelling.
with GSAP ScrollTrigger ↗ · free
Kinetic split-text headlineMedium
The Obys move — your big headline's letters/words animate in one by one, or react on scroll. Turns plain text into a moment.
with GSAP SplitText ↗ · now free
Magnetic cursor & buttonsEasy
Buttons gently "pull" toward the cursor; a custom dot/ring cursor follows along. The Cuberto feel — tiny code, big reaction.
with vanilla JS (CodePen recipes) ↗
3D hero — no codeMedium
Build a real 3D object/scene by dragging in a visual editor, wire it to cursor/scroll, and embed it. 3D without writing WebGL.
with Spline ↗ · free tier
Animated illustrations & UIEasy
Lightweight characters, icons and buttons that loop and react to hover/click. Personality without heavy video.
with Rive ↗ or LottieFiles
Cursor-reactive hero backgroundEasy
Glowing fog/waves/net or particles that ripple toward the mouse — the Lusion "alive background" feeling, drop-in.
with Vanta.js ↗ or tsParticles
Hover-reveal · tilt · marqueeEasy
Image-reveal-on-hover links, 3D tilt cards, infinite logo marquees — small flourishes that signal craft everywhere.
with Codrops tutorials ↗ · copy & adapt
