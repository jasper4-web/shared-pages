Questions

Before you decide.

What exactly do I get for $350?
A custom-designed website (up to 4 pages), an AI chatbot trained on your business that answers customer questions and captures leads, on-page SEO, domain and hosting for the first year, and 90 days of bug fixes and minor updates from our team. All built and deployed in 1–3 days. Want to edit the site yourself? Add the admin dashboard for a one-time $350.
Is there a monthly fee?
No. The beta is a $350 one-time flat fee, paid in full up front. Domain and hosting are included for the first year. After that, the only cost is renewing your domain and hosting on your own. If you added the admin dashboard, you manage the site yourself — otherwise our team handles updates for you.
How long until my site is live?
1–3 days from the time we have everything we need. You'll fill out a questionnaire that gives us a clear picture of your business, your services, and what you want — and we take it from there.
What happens after the 90 days of support?
If you added the admin dashboard, you manage the site yourself — update text, images, and pages anytime. Without it, our team handles your updates at an hourly rate after the 90 days. Either way, your site keeps running and your domain & hosting stay covered through the first year.
What's the Admin Dashboard add-on?
An optional +$350 dashboard for editing and managing your own site — change text, photos, prices, and pages anytime, no code and no waiting on us. It's also where you view and manage your leads — every SANO site captures leads to your inbox whether or not you add the dashboard; this just puts them in one place. Totally optional: skip it and our team makes your changes for you.
What if I need more than 4 pages?
Additional pages beyond the 4 included in the beta are just $75 per page. Most small businesses find 4 pages covers the essentials — home, about, services, and contact.
Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. We build and launch everything for you. If you want to make changes yourself, the optional admin dashboard is as simple as editing a document — if you can type, you can update your site. Prefer not to bother? Just send us the change and we'll handle it.
What's coming after the beta?
We have a lot more planned — think CRM, missed call automation, appointment booking, AI-powered tools, and more. Beta clients get first access to everything we build next. The beta is just the beginning.
